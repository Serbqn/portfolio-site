# Atlas Maps

## Problem

Drivers were using a paper sheet and a personal phone to do a job that needed real-time dispatch updates. Ops had no visibility into what was happening on the ground.

## Process

Two weeks embedded with the dispatch team and the drivers. I tested paper prototypes in the cab, in the rain, with gloves on. The design rules followed from there.

## Solution

A two-pane mobile app: a map on the left, a tappable task list on the right. Every action is one-handed and thumb-reachable. Offline mode handles 4 hours of cell dead zones.

## Results

- Task completion is up 28%.
- Route deviation is down 19%.
- On-shift incidents have nearly halved.
- The app now powers 800+ drivers across three countries.
