# Harbor Payments

## Problem

Marketplace operators were getting a flood of seller questions — "where is my payout?", "why was it delayed?", "is it stuck?" — and the Harbor team had to dig through five different internal tools to answer each one.

## Process

Mapped the seller lifecycle across five tools and identified the four question shapes 80% of tickets fell into. Designed a single payouts console that could answer all four without leaving the page.

## Solution

A dense, table-first console with three views (by payout, by seller, by corridor) and a side panel that opens for any row. The side panel answers the four common questions inline; deeper investigation is one click away.

## Results

- Five tools became one.
- The Harbor support team now resolves most payouts questions without leaving the console.
- The product launched to general availability in Q3 2025.
